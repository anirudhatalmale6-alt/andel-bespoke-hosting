Who We Are

Two experts, one shared mission

Andel & Co. was founded by two industry professionals who saw an opportunity to do things differently. While the short-term rental market boomed, the quality of management lagged behind. We stepped in to bridge that gap.

With deep expertise in operations, client relations, and revenue strategy, we offer a hands-on, consultative approach to co-hosting. We don't just manage listings — we build successful rental businesses.

We keep our client portfolio intentionally small. Why? Because bespoke service demands dedicated attention — and that is precisely what makes us different.
