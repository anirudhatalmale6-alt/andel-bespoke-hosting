The Beginning

Born from a belief that every stay matters

Andel & Co. was founded on a simple observation: the short-term rental market was overflowing with listings, yet genuinely memorable stays remained rare. We saw an opportunity — not just to manage properties, but to craft experiences.

Drawing on years of hospitality expertise, we built a co-hosting practice rooted in the traditions of fine hotel service. Every guest interaction, every listing detail, every pricing decision reflects a commitment to excellence that most property managers simply don't offer.

Today, we partner with a select number of property owners who share our standards. We keep our portfolio intentionally small because bespoke service demands dedicated attention — and that is precisely what your property deserves.
